# KLYTHRA

### AI-Powered Operational Decision Intelligence for Zero-Harm Industrial Operations

> **Predict. Explain. Prevent.**

KLYTHRA is an AI-powered industrial safety intelligence platform that transforms reactive safety monitoring into proactive operational decision-making.

Rather than generating isolated alerts, KLYTHRA continuously analyzes live sensor streams, permit-to-work systems, maintenance schedules, workforce activities, historical incidents, and regulatory standards to predict compound operational risk before accidents occur.

---

## Problem Statement

Industrial facilities generate thousands of safety signals every day.

Traditional monitoring systems detect individual alarms but fail to understand how multiple operational conditions interact to create high-risk scenarios.

This often leads to:

- Delayed decision-making
- Alarm fatigue
- Hidden compound risks
- Regulatory non-compliance
- Preventable industrial incidents

KLYTHRA solves this by becoming the intelligence layer above existing industrial systems.

---

# Key Features

### Operational Intelligence Dashboard

Monitor live operational health across the plant with a unified Compound Risk Index.

---

### Live Plant Digital Twin

Visualize every operational zone, permits, maintenance activities, and sensor streams in real time.

---

### Explainable AI Decision Center

Receive AI-generated operational recommendations with complete reasoning, confidence scores, and expected risk reduction.

---

### Predictive Timeline

Forecast operational risk over the coming hours before incidents occur.

---

### Knowledge Graph

Understand relationships between equipment, permits, workers, hazards, maintenance tasks, sensors, and regulations.

---

### Industrial Memory

Learn from historical incidents using AI-powered pattern recognition and similarity search.

---

### Compliance Intelligence

Automatically map every recommendation against industrial regulations including:

- OISD
- Factories Act
- DGMS

---

### Scenario Simulation

Compare multiple operational strategies before execution and evaluate their projected safety impact.

---

### Audit & Explainability

Every AI-assisted recommendation is permanently recorded with reasoning, timestamps, confidence, and regulatory references.
